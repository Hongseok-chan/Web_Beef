Tenderloin

The Tenderloin is oblong shape spanning two primal cuts: the sortloin and the sirloin. The denderloin sits beneath the ribs, next to the backbone. It has two ends: the butt and the "tail". The smaller, pointed end -the "tail"- starts a little past the ribs, growing in thckness untill it ends in the "sirloin" primal cut, which is closer to the butt of the cow. This muscle does very little work, so it is the tenderest part of the beef.
